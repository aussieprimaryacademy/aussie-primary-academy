Year 1 Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents, tutors and teachers of Year 1 students.

What is Year 1?

Year 1 is the second year of primary school in Australia, following Foundation or Prep. Students build confidence in reading, writing, maths and other core learning areas.

Are Year 1 worksheets aligned with the Australian Curriculum?

Yes, our Year 1 worksheets are written to be Australian Curriculum-aligned and ACARA-aware. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent publisher and is not an official ACARA product or endorsed by ACARA.

Do Year 1 worksheets need adult help?

Many Year 1 worksheets can be completed independently, although some children may still benefit from support with reading instructions or learning new concepts.

Are answer guides included for Year 1 worksheets?

Yes, each Year 1 worksheet comes with a matching answer guide, making it easy for parents, teachers and tutors to check work quickly and accurately.
