About Aussie Primary Academy

Aussie Primary Academy provides free, printable learning resources for Australian primary students from Foundation to Year 6.

Our Story

Aussie Primary Academy was created to make useful, curriculum-aware learning practice easier to access. Families and educators should not need expensive subscriptions or complicated sign-ups to find clear printable activities for primary students.

Our growing worksheet library supports parents, teachers, tutors and homeschool families across Australia. Resources are designed for focused practice at home, in classrooms and during tutoring sessions.

Our Mission

Practical resources that help children learn with confidence.

  • Free Access

    Learning Without Barriers

    Provide free worksheets that families and educators can download without creating an account.

  • Australian Learning

    Curriculum-Aware Practice

    Develop resources informed by the Australian Curriculum V9.0 and suitable for Australian primary learners.

  • Clear Support

    Made for Real Use

    Create print-ready activities with clear instructions and answer support wherever appropriate.

Who Our Resources Support

Our worksheets are created for Australian parents, classroom teachers, tutors and homeschool families supporting children from Foundation to Year 6.

Aussie Primary Academy is an independent educational publisher. Our resources are Australian Curriculum-aligned and ACARA-aware, but they are not official ACARA products and are not endorsed by ACARA.
