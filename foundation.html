Foundation Worksheet FAQs

Helpful information for parents, teachers, tutors and homeschool families.

Are these Foundation worksheets free?

Yes. Every worksheet listed on this page is available as a free printable PDF for educational use at home, at school, in tutoring sessions or in homeschool programs.

Are these worksheets aligned to the Australian Curriculum?

The worksheets are designed to support Australian Curriculum V9.0 Foundation content and skills. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent educational publisher and is not endorsed by ACARA.

What subjects are included?

This Foundation collection includes Maths, English, Reading, Writing, Phonics and Science.

Can parents use these worksheets at home?

Yes. Foundation learners often benefit from adult support. Parents can read instructions aloud and work alongside children during early learning activities.

Are answer guides included?

Each worksheet should be used with its matching answer guide where an answer guide is available. Answer guides help parents, teachers and tutors check student work efficiently.

Will more Foundation worksheets be added?

Yes. More Foundation resources will be added as the Aussie Primary Academy worksheet library grows.
