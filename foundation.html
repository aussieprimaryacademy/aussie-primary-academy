Foundation Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents, tutors and teachers of Foundation students.

What is Foundation (Prep)?

Foundation, also known as Prep in some states, is the first official year of primary school in Australia, before Year 1.

Are Foundation worksheets aligned with the Australian Curriculum?

Yes, our Foundation worksheets are written to be Australian Curriculum-aligned and ACARA-aware. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent publisher and is not an official ACARA product or endorsed by ACARA.

Do Foundation worksheets need adult help?

Most Foundation worksheets are designed for an adult, carer or teacher to sit alongside the child, especially for reading instructions and modelling new skills.

Are answer guides included for Foundation worksheets?

Some Foundation worksheets include answer guides, while others focus on hands-on activities such as tracing, matching and colouring. Each worksheet page clearly indicates whether an answer guide is included.
