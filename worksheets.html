A note for parents and teachers

Every worksheet on this page is designed to be printed and used straight away — no sign-up, no account and no ongoing cost. Each one includes an answer guide, so you can check work quickly whether you're supporting one child at the kitchen table or a full classroom.

Worksheets are written to be Australian Curriculum-aligned and ACARA-aware, making them a useful supplement to classroom teaching, tutoring sessions or homeschool programs. They're not a substitute for a full teaching program, but a handy way to add focused, low-prep practice wherever it's needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents, tutors and teachers.

Are the worksheets free?

Yes. Every worksheet on Aussie Primary Academy is free to download and print, with no sign-up required.

Are they Australian Curriculum-aligned?

Our worksheets are written to be Australian Curriculum-aligned and ACARA-aware. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent publisher and is not an official ACARA product or endorsed by ACARA.

Do worksheets include answer guides?

Yes. Every worksheet comes with a matching answer guide, so parents and tutors can check work without needing to solve it themselves first.

Can teachers use them in classrooms?

Yes. Teachers are welcome to print and use these worksheets for classroom activities, homework or extension tasks.
