Year 6 Worksheet FAQs

Helpful information for teachers, parents, tutors and homeschool families.

Are these Year 6 worksheets free?

Yes. Every worksheet listed on this page is available as a free printable PDF for educational use at home, at school, in tutoring sessions or in homeschool programs.

Are these worksheets aligned to the Australian Curriculum?

The worksheets are designed to support Australian Curriculum V9.0 Year 6 content and skills. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent educational publisher and is not endorsed by ACARA.

Do these worksheets help with NAPLAN preparation?

Year 6 builds the literacy and numeracy foundations students will use in secondary school and later NAPLAN testing. Our reading comprehension, grammar and maths worksheets provide useful general practice, although they are not official NAPLAN test materials.

What subjects are included?

This Year 6 collection currently includes Maths, English and Science. English includes grammar, reading comprehension and writing activities.

Will more Year 6 worksheets be added?

Yes. New Year 6 resources will be added as the library grows, including additional maths and science topics.
