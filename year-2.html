Year 2 Worksheet FAQs

Helpful information for teachers, parents, tutors and homeschool families.

Are these Year 2 worksheets free?

Yes. Every worksheet listed on this page is available as a free printable PDF for educational use at home, at school, in tutoring sessions or in homeschool programs.

Are these worksheets aligned to the Australian Curriculum?

The worksheets are designed to support Australian Curriculum V9.0 Year 2 content and skills. Aussie Primary Academy is an independent educational publisher and is not endorsed by ACARA.

What subjects are included?

This Year 2 collection currently includes Maths, English and Science. English includes grammar, punctuation, reading comprehension and writing activities.

Can parents use these worksheets at home?

Yes. The clear instructions and focused activities make them suitable for homework, revision, extra practice and homeschool learning.

Will more Year 2 worksheets be added?

Yes. New Year 2 resources will be added as the library grows, including additional reading and curriculum topics.
